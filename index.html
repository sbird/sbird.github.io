Research

I am a McWilliams Fellow at Carnegie Mellon University, and was formerly at the Institute for Advanced Study. For my PhD I studied with Hiranya Peiris and Martin Haehnelt at the IoA, Cambridge.

I am interested in various aspects of theoretical and numerical cosmology, in particular simulating massive neutrinos, the Lyman-alpha forest and the implications of inflation.
